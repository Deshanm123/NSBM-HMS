"NSBM Eco-System"

NSBM Green University is an educational institute that has escaped from its limits and developed up to an area of 26 acres ecosystem. NSBM Green University was built with the intention of appreciation of nature and to provide the best educational experience one can receive. NSBM Green University Town is the first ever Green University built in South Asian Region and it sets an example for the whole world by paving the way for environment sustainability.

NSBM Hostel Complex

On-campus accomodation is available at NSBM Green University Town premises.
student dormitories are made with international standards giving students the best comfort, relaxation and convenience.
each accomodation unit is equipped with all all necessary facilities required for the students and the allocation will be done on twin-sharing basis.

  • Facilities And Features:
    • fully furnished rooms with bed, table and chair wardrobe and towel rack.
    • Split type air conditioner unit for each room.
    • Attached bathroom in international standards.
    • On premises restaurant open 365 days for food and beverages with local and international menus at subsidized prices.
    • Common and private study areas.
    • Keyless entry secured access control system for rooms through NFC Electronic Cards.
    • Free wifi access for each room and study / common areas.
    • Laundry facilities on the premises.
    • Secured hostel premises with onsite security personnel on 24x7 basis and CCTV monitoring systems.
    • Customized rooms for differently abled students.
    • Private balcony with panoramic view of the nature and fresh natural air.
    • Free AC, electricity power and drinking water.
    • Elevator and staircase access to all floors.
    A dedicated warden and an academic warden are available on premises with fulltime access to students.
    Students are required to bring all personal belongings required for living away from home for a substantial period including pillows, blankets, and bed sheets.
